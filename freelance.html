Case studies

2023-24 Interaction Design and User Research for ILF Scotland

We started by designing an online service for social workers to make applications to ILF on behalf of citizens. We tested a prototype on social workers then passed this over to developers to build the real service. We also ran an accessibility audit to identify any issues which might stop disabled people from using the service.

We're currently running interviews for ILF for a Discovery project.

2022-23 Content Design for Wilshire

We helped the international financial data company Wilshire launch their new Sharepoint intranet. We engaged with different company stakeholders to capture their requirements. We then designed a new look and feel based on the company structure and an existing stock of photography.

2021-22 Interaction Design for Calba

Working with Calba, we designed a prototype version of a new online application form for a small Scottish government body. We tested this on users and made iterative improvements to the content and design. We passed a version of the prototype to developers to build the real online service.

We ran an accessibility audit to identify any issues which might stop people from applying online. We then wrote JIRA tickets to the development team to fix them.
