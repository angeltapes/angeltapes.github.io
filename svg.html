About Shoestring UI

A collection of accessible code I've put together. I use it to make quick prototypes of web pages, apps and software.

Not much of of this is original. I've taken code from Heydon Pickering's book Inclusive Design Patterns as well as the W3C schools website.

Hire Madmarch to turn your ideas into prototypes. We can help you test them out on real people.

You can also contact me on LinkedIn to discuss your project. I'm Bobby King, a designer based in Glasgow. Cheers!
